## What Is Melanated In Tech?

Melanated In Tech (MIT) is a Global AI Agent and Emerging Technology Platform founded for Black and melanated professionals, entrepreneurs, and creators who are building wealth through AI. The platform is open to all who share the mission of economic empowerment through technology.

The platform vision, in five words: **Learn. Build. Deploy. Scale. Monetize.**

---

## Where We Are Now

The current melanatedintech.com is an AI tools discovery directory built on Next.js + Supabase. It contains:

- 38+ curated AI tools organized into 10 categories
- A basic blog with a small number of articles
- An about page, contact page, legal pages
- Email newsletter signup
- User authentication (sign-in/favorites)
- A tool submission form

The current domain is melanatedintech.com but the sitemap and robots.txt still reference the legacy domain vaultxaitools.com. The rebrand is partially complete.

The current platform is a **directory**, not yet a **platform**. There is no community, no courses, no marketplace, no certifications, no paid products beyond potential consulting.

---

## Where We Are Going

Melanated In Tech will become a full-stack AI ecosystem with five interconnected pillars:

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| **Academy** | Courses, tutorials, certifications, and learning tracks on AI agents, MCP servers, prompt engineering, automation, and AI business building |
| **Marketplace** | A curated marketplace where builders list and sell AI agents, MCP servers, agent skills, prompt packs, and blueprints |
| **Community** | A peer network of Black and melanated AI builders — structured, accountable, and designed for collaboration |
| **Consulting** | An agency layer that places community builders inside enterprise client engagements |
| **Content** | SEO-anchored editorial content, tutorials, and use-case pages that drive organic growth |

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Black and melanated professionals, entrepreneurs, developers, and creators who want to build AI-powered businesses and income streams.

**Secondary:** All professionals who believe in economic empowerment through technology ownership — regardless of background.

**Psychographic:** People who are done consuming AI content and ready to build with it. Technical ambition + business intent + community orientation.

## Business Model Summary

Five revenue engines:

1. **Memberships** — Free, Community ($29/mo), Builder ($79/mo), Pro ($149/mo), Enterprise (custom)
2. **Marketplace** — 15–30% commission on third-party product sales
3. **Courses & Certifications** — $49–$999 per product
4. **Consulting & Enterprise** — $5K–$50K per engagement
5. **Events & Sponsorships** — Summits, hackathons, brand partnerships
