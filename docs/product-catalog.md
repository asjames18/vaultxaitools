## Product Launch Sequence

### Phase 1 — Months 1–2 (Foundation)
**Goal:** Revenue-generating from day one. Build trust, collect proof.

1. Launch 3 Prompt Packs (Black Entrepreneur Starter, AI Agency Sales Playbook, Content Creator Accelerator)
2. Launch 2 Courses (AI Tools for Non-Technical Professionals + ChatGPT Masterclass)
3. Launch Membership Free + Community tiers

### Phase 2 — Months 3–5 (Product Depth)
**Goal:** Convert audience into higher-value buyers.

4. Launch 5 AI Agents (ContentFlow, CareerCoach, ColdEmail, MeetingMind, PodcastPro)
5. Launch 3 Blueprints (YouTube-to-Content Flywheel, Client Onboarding Machine, Sales Pipeline)
6. Launch Builder Membership Tier
7. Launch Prompt Engineering Fundamentals + Build Your First AI Agent courses

### Phase 3 — Months 6–9 (Marketplace)
**Goal:** Shift from creator-only to platform.

8. Launch top 5 MCP Servers (Notion, Airtable, Google Workspace, GitHub, Slack)
9. Open Third-Party Marketplace (invite 3–5 verified sellers)
10. Launch Pro Membership Tier
11. Launch 5 more courses (n8n, AI for Black Business, Monetize AI, AI Ethics, Supabase + AI)

### Phase 4 — Months 10–18 (Scale)
**Goal:** Land enterprise clients, complete the catalog.

12. Launch Enterprise Tier + Consulting Offer
13. Launch remaining AI Agents and MCP Servers
14. Launch Advanced Courses (RAG, Voice AI, AI Consultant Playbook, Corporate Cohort)
15. Launch White-Label and Licensing Program
