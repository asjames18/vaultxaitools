## SEO Technical Priorities

1. **Own the MCP cluster now** — publish cornerstone + 3 tutorials in Week 1–2
2. **Semantic relevance signaling** — use related terms naturally, not just exact-match keywords
3. **Structured data** — FAQPage schema on cornerstones, HowTo on tutorials, Article on blog posts
4. **Internal link equity** — all spokes link to their hub pillar page
5. **Content freshness** — update cornerstones quarterly with new data and examples
6. **Featured snippet optimization** — for "how to" and "what is" queries, structure content with clear question H2 + direct answer paragraph
7. **People Also Ask** — identify PAA questions for each target keyword and answer them in content
8. **Search Console monitoring** — weekly review of crawl errors, impressions, clicks, position changes
