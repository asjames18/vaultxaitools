# Business Model — Melanated In Tech

> **North Star:** Melanated In Tech is building the world's most practical ecosystem for AI Agents, MCP Servers, Agent Skills, Automation Systems, and Agent Commerce. Every decision should move the platform closer to becoming the trusted place where people learn, build, deploy, buy, sell, and scale AI-powered systems.

---

## Model Type

**Multi-sided platform** connecting:
- Learners (students consuming Academy content)
- Builders (community members building and selling)
- Buyers (anyone purchasing from the Marketplace)
- Employers/Clients (enterprises hiring through Consulting)

Each side creates value for the others. Community members graduate from Academy → build products → sell in Marketplace → attract new learners → grow community.

## Pricing Psychology

- **Free tier** exists to build trust and demonstrate value — not to retain non-buyers
- **Annual discount** (2 months free) drives commitment and reduces churn
- **Course pricing** anchored against Udemy ($15–$30) at premium but justified by depth and marketplace outcome
- **Consulting** priced against boutique AI agencies ($10K–$50K), justified by cultural trust + quality
- **Never discount publicly** — use cohort pricing, launch pricing, and founding member rates as scarcity mechanisms

## Key Risks to This Model

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Low initial course sales | Launch with free content → demonstrate value → convert |
| Marketplace doesn't reach liquidity | MIT seeds it with 10 own products first |
| Consulting scales poorly | Cap at 5 active engagements; use community talent to expand |
| High churn on memberships | Community depth and peer relationships are the retention moat |
| Stripe payout issues | Test thoroughly in Stripe test mode; manual invoice fallback |
