## What Is This Platform?

**Melanated In Tech** is a global AI agent and emerging technology platform for Black and melanated professionals, entrepreneurs, developers, and creators who want to **build wealth through AI ownership** — not just use AI tools someone else built.

The platform sits at the intersection of:
- **Education** (Academy — deep, practical AI curriculum)
- **Monetization** (Marketplace — sell agents, MCP servers, skills, prompts)
- **Community** (Peer network of AI builders, not passive learners)
- **Infrastructure** (Deployment-ready tools, not just theory)
- **Consulting** (Enterprise contracts sourced from and delivered by the community)

---

## The Problem It Solves

The AI economy is creating massive wealth. But without deliberate intervention, this wealth transfer happens **without Black ownership participation**. Most AI education teaches people to *use* AI tools. It does not teach them to *build* AI tools, *deploy* AI agents, or *monetize* AI systems.

Melanated In Tech closes this gap by being the platform that takes someone from **AI curious → AI sovereign**.

## The Five Pillars

### 1. Academy
Deep, practical curriculum on the actual infrastructure of AI:
- AI Agent Architecture (ReAct, Plan-and-Execute, multi-agent systems)
- MCP Servers (building, deploying, monetizing)
- Prompt Engineering (advanced patterns, not basic ChatGPT tips)
- Automation Workflows (n8n, Make, custom Python)
- Local AI (Ollama, LM Studio, privacy-first deployment)
- Agent Skills & Tools (Claude Code, Playwright, search tools)
- AI Business Solutions (packaging, pricing, finding clients)
- Agent Fleets & Enterprise AI (scaling intelligence)

**Products:** Courses ($49–$999), Certifications, Bootcamps, Workshops, Free tracks

### 2. Marketplace
Curated digital marketplace where community builders **list and sell** their AI work:
- AI Agents ($19–$499)
- MCP Servers ($29–$299)
- Prompt Packs ($19–$99)
- Agent Skills ($9–$79)
- Agent Blueprints ($49–$499)
- Bundles ($99–$599)

**Commission structure:** 30/70 (Standard) → 10/90 (Exclusive Partner)

### 3. Community
Structured peer network of builders:
- Forums (help, showcase, tools, career, general)
- Live events and workshops
- Member profiles with seller reputation
- Challenges and leaderboard gamification

### 4. Consulting
Enterprise AI consulting agency layer:
- Community talent sourced for client engagements
- Founder QA's deliverables, manages client relationships
- Retainers: $5K–$50K/month
- White-label licensing to HBCUs, workforce dev, corporate DEI

### 5. Content
SEO-anchored editorial and tutorial engine:
- 8 content pillars mapped to high-intent keywords
- Tools directory (38+ tools, growing)
- Newsletter ("The Stack") — weekly, 800–1,200 words
- YouTube, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, TikTok distribution
