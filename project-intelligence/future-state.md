## The Technology in 2029

The platform has evolved through three infrastructure phases:

**Current architecture:**
- Next.js 15 App Router (still — RSC architecture aging well)
- Supabase Enterprise (multi-region, read replicas, vector search active)
- Clerk (auth, org management for Enterprise tier)
- Stripe (subscriptions, Connect, Billing, Tax)
- Cloudflare R2 (product delivery)
- Typesense (full-text search, 1.5M+ documents indexed)
- pgvector (semantic search for marketplace, course, and community content)
- Inngest (background jobs — email sequences, analytics, marketplace operations)
- Mux (course video hosting, 2M+ video views)
- ClickHouse (analytics warehouse, real-time dashboards)
- Redis/Upstash (session caching, rate limiting)

**New capabilities:**

- AI-Powered Recommendations — personalized course and product recommendations via pgvector embeddings
