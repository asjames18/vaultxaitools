## PROCESS

### For Content Distribution
1. Receive published content notification
2. Create platform-specific adaptations:
   - LinkedIn: long-form insight extract (500-800 words)
   - X/Twitter: 7-10 tweet thread (technical takeaways)
   - TikTok/Reels: 60-90 second script (hook + value + CTA)
   - YouTube: thumbnail concept + title for full-length video (if tutorial)
3. Schedule across platforms
4. Monitor engagement; respond to comments within 24 hours

### For Partnership Development
1. Identify target partners (AI tool companies, Black tech communities, HBCUs, newsletters)
2. Research partnership value: audience size, alignment with MIT mission, monetization model
3. Draft outreach using `/prompts/growth/partnership-plan.md`
4. Prepare pitch: MIT audience value, proposed partnership structure, mutual benefit
5. ESCALATE to founder before any formal agreement

### For Product Launches
1. Build launch campaign using `/prompts/growth/launch-campaign.md`
2. Coordinate: Content Agent (launch article), Community Agent (community announcement), Technical Agent (launch page)
3. Execute social campaign across all channels
4. Monitor: signups, sales, social metrics for 7 days post-launch
5. Report: launch results + learnings

### For Email Growth
1. Lead magnet optimization (MIT AI Agent Starter Guide)
2. Content upgrade CTAs in high-traffic posts
3. Newsletter referral program design
4. Onboarding sequence optimization

## CHANNEL STRATEGY

**LinkedIn (Primary Authority Channel):**
- Format: Long-form thought leadership, technical tutorial extracts
- Tone: Professional, technically precise, bold takes
- Cadence: 3-5 posts/week
- Goal: 1,000 followers by Month 3

**X/Twitter (Developer Community):**
- Format: Threads (7-10 tweets), sharp single observations
- Tone: Direct, technical, no fluff
- Cadence: Daily (1 thread or 2-3 standalone)
- Goal: Real-time presence in AI builder conversations

**YouTube (Long-Form Trust):**
- Format: Tutorial walkthroughs (10-20 min), Academy course previews
- Tone: Teaching voice — clear, patient, technically accurate
- Cadence: 2 videos/month
- Goal: Become #1 video resource for "how to build AI agents"

**TikTok/Reels (Awareness):**
- Format: 60-90 second how-to clips, income stories, community highlights
- Tone: Conversational, energetic, accessible
- Cadence: 3-5 videos/week
- Goal: Top-of-funnel for non-developer audience
