## EMAIL GROWTH LEVERS

### Lever 1: Lead Magnet (Primary Growth Driver)
**Asset:** MIT AI Agent Starter Guide
- 20-page PDF
- Contents: What agents are, how to build first one, 10 use cases, toolkit list
- Gate: Email signup at signup prompt, in-content CTAs, and homepage

**Placement:**
- Homepage newsletter section
- End of every blog post / tutorial
- Exit-intent popup (Phase 2)
- Social bio links

**Optimization:**
- A/B test headline: "MIT AI Agent Starter Guide" vs. "Build Your First AI Agent (Free Guide)"
- A/B test form placement: below post vs. mid-post
- Track conversion rate: visitor → lead magnet download

### Lever 2: Content Upgrades (Per-Post)
For each major tutorial, create a content upgrade:
- "Download this tutorial as a PDF"
- "Get the code for this tutorial"
- "Download the prompt templates from this post"

**Implementation:** Resend-gated download link (requires email)

### Lever 3: Newsletter Value (The Stack)
The Stack must be good enough that readers forward it. That forward becomes the best ad MIT will ever run.

Weekly send must include:
- 1 thing worth sharing (viral potential)
- 1 tutorial preview (drives click)
- 1 community win (social proof)

**Referral program (Phase 3):** Subscribers earn rewards for referring new subscribers.

### Lever 4: Social-to-Email
Every social post with strong engagement gets a follow-up:
- Twitter: "Want the full guide? [link to lead magnet]"
- LinkedIn: "Full tutorial in the comments / bio link"
- TikTok: "Link in bio — free starter guide"

### Lever 5: Community Onboarding
Every new community member → newsletter signup (not optional for Community+ tier)
