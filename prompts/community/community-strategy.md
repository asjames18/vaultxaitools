# Community Strategy — Melanated In Tech

> **North Star:** Melanated In Tech is building the world's most practical ecosystem for AI Agents, MCP Servers, Agent Skills, Automation Systems, and Agent Commerce. Every decision should move the platform closer to becoming the trusted place where people learn, build, deploy, buy, sell, and scale AI-powered systems.

---

## PURPOSE
Define and maintain the MIT community strategy — the principles, architecture, and programming that make the community the most valuable part of the platform.

---

## COMMUNITY PHILOSOPHY

**Community is not a feature. It is the product.**

The MIT community is the reason members stay after they've consumed the content. It's the accountability structure that turns course enrollments into deployed agents. It's the talent pool that feeds the consulting arm. It's the social proof that converts the next generation of members.

**The MIT community is a high-performance environment, not a support group.**

MIT builders ship things. They get accountability from peers. They celebrate real wins — not participation. They help each other with specificity, not generic encouragement.

## COMMUNITY PROGRAMMING

### Weekly Rhythm
| Day | Activity | Owner |
|-----|----------|-------|
| Monday | "What are you building?" thread | Community Agent |
| Wednesday | Member Spotlight | Community Agent |
| Friday | "Show your work" showcase | Community Agent |

### Monthly Programming
| Week | Activity |
|------|---------|
| Week 1 | New member welcome event (intro session) |
| Week 2 | Live workshop (skills-focused, 60-90 min) |
| Week 3 | AMA with community member who shipped something |
| Week 4 | Builder League challenge close / new cohort kick-off |

### MIT Builder League (Accountability Cohorts)
- 5-8 members per cohort
- 90-day cohorts: specific goal (launch product, earn $1K, complete cert)
- Weekly async check-in thread
- Community Agent facilitates matching; members self-direct
- Launch: Phase 3 (after 500+ active members)

## COMMUNITY STANDARDS (enforcement guide)

| Standard | How to Enforce |
|----------|---------------|
| Show your work | Model the behavior; request specifics when too vague |
| Be specific | Respond to vague posts with a clarifying question |
| Celebrate others | Feature wins in Wednesday spotlight |
| No spam | 1 warning → 7-day mute → permanent ban |
| Respect the mission | Escalate undermining behavior to founder |

**Moderation philosophy:** Clear expectations → consistent enforcement → generous grace for new members on first offense.
