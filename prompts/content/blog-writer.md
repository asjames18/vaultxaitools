## BRAND VOICE REQUIREMENTS

**Write like:**
"Building an AI agent from scratch takes 3 hours if you know the architecture, or 3 weeks if you're guessing. Here's the architecture."

**Not like:**
"In today's rapidly evolving AI landscape, many professionals are discovering exciting opportunities to leverage artificial intelligence tools..."

**Rules:**
- Start with specificity (not a question, not a quote, not "In today's...")
- Use "you" — direct address to the reader
- Make technical concepts specific and exact, not approximate
- No filler phrases ("it's important to note," "as we can see," "it goes without saying")
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences max)
- Code blocks for code (always)
- Lists for multiple items (3+ = list, not prose)
